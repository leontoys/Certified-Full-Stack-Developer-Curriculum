Ingredients

  • 1/8 lb of kingfish, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 2 tapioca tubers, (kappa), washed, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 oz of coconut, grated
  • 1 tbsp of coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the 'kappa', or tapioca tuber.
  2. Grind the grated coconut, chillies, onion and turmeric powder together with a pinch of salt.
  3. Place a small wok over a medium-high heat and add the oil.
  4. To prepare the curry, clean the kingfish and cut into medium-sized pieces
